## About

I'm an Electrical Engineering student at the German University in Cairo, with a background in frontend web development, cybersecurity, and hardware prototyping — built mostly through self-learning and hands-on work in Gaza.

For 9 years I co-led science and technology initiatives at the Abdel Mohsen Al-Qattan Foundation, where I built real engineering projects from scratch under serious resource constraints. I competed in IEEEXtreme 18.0 during the war — no stable power, no reliable internet — and our team still finished in the **global top 1,000 out of 19,000+ participants**.

Gaza taught me that creativity is a survival skill. I bring that same mindset to every technical problem I work on.
